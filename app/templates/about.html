Welcome to Squawker!

Background

Squawker is a web-based application that allows you to divide your Twitter timeline into categories based on who you follow. For example, you can group the people you follow into "news", "sports", "friends", and any other category you might think of!

Your timeline is filtered to only show tweets from whichever category you want to see.

Login

To get started, sign into Twitter using the button below. This will redirect you to Twitter's login page.

Returning User

Been here before? If you've been here before on a different browser or have cleared your cookies, Squawker will remember the setup you've created before. Just sign into Twitter and your previous settings will be restored.

Data & Privacy

No personal data is collected by this application. Login credentials are given directly to Twitter using the secure OAuth protocol and your information is never shared with this application. For more information on the OAuth protocal used by Twitter, please read here.
